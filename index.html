Honoring Our Ancestors

Welcome to the Santa Rosa First Peoples Community. We are the direct descendants of Trinidad's original Amerindian inhabitants, dedicated to preserving our sacred heritage in the heart of Arima.

Who We Are

The Heartbeat of Indigenous Trinidad


Established in 1976, the Santa Rosa First Peoples Community is the primary organization representing Indigenous people in Trinidad and Tobago. Located in the municipality of Arima, a land deeply rooted in our history, we work tirelessly to ensure the survival of our traditions.


From the sacred Smoke Ceremony honoring the Great Spirit, to the intricate art of weaving terite reed, our community is a living testament to the resilience of the Aruaca, Nepuyos, Carinepagoto, and other tribes who have called this island home for thousands of years.
